Our Classes

From high-intensity cardio to relaxing yoga, find the class that's right for you.

Yoga

Improve flexibility, strength, and mind with our yoga classes suitable for all levels.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Burn calories and improve cardiovascular health with short bursts of intense exercise followed by recovery periods.

Strength Training

Build muscle, increase strength, and improve bone density with our comprehensive strength training program.

Spin

Pilates

Enhance your flexibility, strength, and body awareness with Pilates, a workout that emphasizes proper postural alignment and core strength.

Boxing

Get into fighting shape with our boxing classes, combining cardio and strength training for a full-body workout.
